About Me

Having the brain of an engineer, heart of the designer and speech of a diplomat, I specialize in using data and analytics to identify problems and craft simple solutions to address them. My interest in Product Management piqued when I was pursuing my Masters at University of Washington, Seattle.

Working as a Product Manager at Citrix, I am responsible for building features that drive cloud adoption and increase customer engagement.

My obsession with customers started when I built my first product called GradnGo. It bombed massively due to an unsustainable business model but the lessons I learnt have indeed made me think holistically about a Product.

When I am not working, I am either hiking or visiting new places. After coming to United States I feel more connected to nature, thanks to the lush green landscape of Pacific Northwest.
