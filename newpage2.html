Scale and Performance for MCS on AWS

Context: Infrastructure                                   Duration : 4 weeks                            Team-members: Citrix Dev Team

Problem Statement:
With a sudden uptick in number of workloads being migrated to cloud, customers demanded faster provisioning and power management operations

Process:
Covid-19 forced millions of people to work remotely and imposed a huge stress on resources. It proved to be the catalyst in migrating workloads to cloud. With that in mind we received a lot of requests for faster provisioning and power management operations. To support our customers we started testing Machine Creation Services (MCS) on AWS. After engaging with Amazon, understanding their workflow and and increasing our quota limits we are now able to provision and power manage 1500 VMs on AWS. The following operations were performed while testing:

Operation Time (Minutes)
Create 2 catalogs 10
Provision 750 machines in each catalog 249
Start all machines 33
Stop all machines 33
Update Catalogs' image 6
Post Update Start all machines 35
Delete Catalogs 34
